What is our project?

We are testing if colour and saturation values of video games affect behaviour. Our test will involve you playing snake. You will randomly be assigned either a normal version, or one with changed colours. Afterwards, please fill out the survey.

How can I participate?

You can participate by starting to fill out the beginning of the survey. After that, please start playing snake until the timer is up, and continue the survey.
